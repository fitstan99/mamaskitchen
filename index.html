Our Story

Cooking with
heart and soul.

This isn't a
corporate kitchen.

Mama's Kitchen was born out of a simple idea: busy people in Provincetown deserve home-cooked meals without the hassle of grocery shopping and hours in the kitchen.

Run by Stan & Snow, and powered by the one they simply call "Mom" — our chef, our heart, and the reason every meal tastes like it came from someone who genuinely cares.

Every week, Mom cooks fresh from scratch using organic proteins, wild-caught seafood, and quality ingredients — never frozen, never reheated from last week. Just real food, made on demand, for your real life.

We're Provincetown's only dedicated meal prep service — and we're just getting started.
